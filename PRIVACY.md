# Privacy Policy for PayWatch

**Last updated**: November 29, 2024

## Overview

PayWatch is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**PayWatch does NOT collect any personal data.**

We do not:
- Collect any personal information
- Track your usage
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Share any information with third parties

## Data Storage

PayWatch stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- Your salary settings (amount, type, currency)
- Your work schedule (hours and days)
- Your language preference
- Your manual mode status

This data is stored using Chrome's Storage API and never leaves your device.

## Permissions

PayWatch requires only one permission:

### Storage Permission
Used to save your settings (salary, work hours, preferences) locally on your device. This allows your settings to persist between browser sessions.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, the security of your data is managed by Chrome's built-in security features.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- All data can be cleared by uninstalling the extension
- You can modify your settings at any time
- No data is stored outside your local device

---

**Summary**: PayWatch is a privacy-first extension. We don't collect, track, or share anything. All your data stays on your device.
